PRIVACY POLICY

About Policy

Konnectify is a leading and committed logo, website, animation, and stationery design firm. At Konnectify, it’s our prime concern to value your privacy and build trust.

Our privacy commitments

Our privacy commitments are indispensable to the way we do business every day. These apply to everyone who has a relationship with us – including Clients (logo, website, animation, and stationery) and Website visitors. This is how our privacy commitments work:

Secure Personal Information

We keep your personal information safe and we always protect your privacy. We use encryption and other security safeguards to protect your data.

Clients Loyalty

We never sell your personal information for any purpose, to any third party.

Understandable Language

We fully disclose our privacy policy in a simple language that can be understandable to anyone.

No Third Party Commitment

Konnectify has choices to use your information for marketing purposes, only after your approval.

24/7 Customer Support

To provide the best services, we want to hear from you. You can send us queries or feedback on our privacy policy.

Personal Data We Collect

Konnectify collects data to work effectively and deliver you the best experiences with our services. You provide some of this data directly, such as when you create a Konnectify account, submit a query on website design, ask to make an animated video, upload a document to make a brochure, or contact us for support.

We also use services from other companies to help us determine a location based on your IP address in order to customize certain services to your location. The data we collect depends on the services and features you acquire, and includes the following:

Basic Information

Your business name, email address, and contact preferences.

Your name, contact details, and other personal information.

Credentials

We collect passwords and similar security information used for authentication and account access.

Demographic Information

We collect data about you such as your country and preferred language.

Content

We collect content of your work and infrastructures when necessary to provide you with the services you use. This includes: the taglines of your logos, the content of your website, brochure, stationery, video, and other work-related documents. It also includes the content of your communications sent or received, such as the:

  • Subject line and body of an email
  • Text or other content of an instant message
  • Audio recording and transcript of a voice message you receive or a text message you dictate

Additionally, when you contact us, such as for customer support, telephonic conversations, or chat sessions with our representatives, may be recorded and monitored.

You have choices about the information we collect. When you are asked to provide personal information, you may decline. But if you choose not to provide information that is necessary to provide a service, you may not be able to use some features or services.

Payment Data

We collect data that is necessary to process your payment if you make purchases, such as your credit card number (payment instrument number), and the security code associated with your payment instrument.

Usage Data

We collect data about how you experience with our services. This includes data, such as the web pages you visit, the search terms you enter, the features you use, and the items you purchase. This also includes data about your device, about the network, regional and language settings, including IP address, operating system, device identifiers, browser, or other software you use to connect with our services. And it also includes data about the performance of our services and any complications you experience with them.

How Konnectify Use Your Information

We use your information in connection with providing our best services to you and, on your behalf, to your targeted visitors and customers. This includes providing you with customer support, which requires us to access your data to assist you There are other specific ways we use your information are listed below:

To manage our services

We may use your data for our internal purposes to create and provide new services, features, or content.

To interact you about your project

We occasionally send you communications regarding our services and your project (e.g. announcements, billing-related matters, welcome email and wishes, changes to our services or policies).

To contact you for marketing purposes

We will only do this if you have consented to our contacting you for this purpose. For example, sharing your project on our website, using your testimonials, etc.

To respond to legal appeals and prevent harm

If we obtain a subpoena or other legal request from legal professionals, we may need to inspect the information we hold to dictate how to respond.

Cookies

We use cookies on Konnectify website. Cookies are small tiny files that are stored on a user’s device that you use to access our services. Each cookie has an expiry date after a certain period of time, depending on how and what we use it for. We use cookies for several reasons:

  • To make our site easier to use.
  • For security reasons.
  • To provide you with personalized content.
  • To improve our services.
  • To advertise to you for better services.

Konnectify does not hold any rights of the work created. This provision is only permissible when the final payment has been received from the client.

Our clients are free to register their logo created by Konnectify under their company name or trademark. However, Konnectify would not be responsible for the infringement of any design. We neither responsibility for an event of a plagiarized name nor we provide registration services to our clients.

Children Provisions

Konnectify services are not proposed for and may not permissibly be used by individuals under the age of 13. If it comes to our knowledge that we have collected information from such a person, we may delete this data without any notice.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We will notify you of revisions to our privacy policy, in advance. We will also post the revised policies here, with an updated revision date.

Contact Information

If you have queries, concerns, or suggestions related to our Privacy Policy and provisions, you may contact us via phone, chat, or email.
